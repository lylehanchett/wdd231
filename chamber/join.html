NP Membership Benefits

Designed for non-profit organizations with no annual fee.

  • Basic directory listing with contact information
  • Access to networking events and workshops
  • Opportunities to promote community service projects
  • Inclusion in quarterly community impact newsletter

Bronze Membership Benefits

Perfect for small businesses getting started in the community.

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Ribbon-cutting ceremony with social media coverage
  • Priority listing in the online directory search
  • Access to member-only marketing resources

Silver Membership Benefits

Ideal for growing businesses increasing their visibility.

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Business spotlight on the chamber home page
  • Discounted sponsorship rates for chamber events
  • Quarterly strategic networking introductions

Gold Membership Benefits

Best for organizations seeking maximum exposure and influence.

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Premium rotating homepage spotlight placement
  • Invitation to executive roundtables and leadership forums
  • Recognition at annual chamber events
