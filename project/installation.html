Professional Installation of Automated Systems

Our Installation Process

DLB Integrators specializes in installing automated conveyors, robotics, stainless-steel washdown equipment, and integrated machine systems. Every installation is performed with precision to minimize downtime and ensure seamless integration with your production line.

DLB Integrators is now Wulftec certified for installation and service!

Installation Capabilities

  • Robotic system installation and calibration
  • Conveyor system integration and safety setup
  • Electrical wiring, control panels, and PLC I/O verification
  • Washdown-rated equipment mounting and sanitation compliance
  • Sensors, scanners, and inspection equipment installation
  • Mechanical assembly, alignment, and equipment leveling

How Our Installations Work

1. Planning & Assessment

We evaluate your production line, understand your goals, and map out the most efficient integration strategy for your new equipment.

2. Mechanical & Electrical Installation

Our technicians install and align equipment, run wiring, test control signals, and verify all PLC communication channels.

3. Testing & Verification

We run your system end-to-end, ensure full washdown compliance, and make sure your equipment is production-ready on day one.
